OUR STORY


Robert Anglero founded D.A.R.T. in 2013, which was a student in the department of Mechanical Engineering at that time. Since then, D.A.R.T. has competed two times in the NASA Student Launch competition. Unfortunately, the first competition the team could not assist because of financial issues. However, the team completed and presented every report needed for the competition and for that NASA gave the team the Rookie Award. The second competition the team assisted the event and had a successful rocket launch.

MISSION & VISION


The students in the Dynamic Aerospace Rocketry Team have a mission to design, build and launch a reusable high power rocket with a scientific or engineering payload. At the same time, promote the development of rocket science and the hobby throughout our university and community.

The vision is to develop future professional engineers and leaders by giving members the opportunity to work on a competitive and complex project. Members will implement their engineering knowledge, and develop communication, time management, and technical skills, while provided with hands on, organized and team structured experience.
