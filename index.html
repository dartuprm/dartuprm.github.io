WHAT IS DART?


Dynamic Aerospace Rocketry Team is a group of N.S.P.E. members (students) committed to participate in different competitions involving rocket science. Students in D.A.R.T. have a mission to design, build and launch a reusable high power rocket with a scientific or engineering payload.

Also, they are looking to develop skills and acquire knowledge in rocket science in different areas such as structures, avionics, propulsion, recovery, and business. At the same time, reach out to other students and high school students to create or amplify the area of interest in rocketry.
